Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This application is operated by Japan Runway Club for the purpose of uploading our own pre-produced video content to our own connected social media accounts, including TikTok.

What we access

When a TikTok account is connected via TikTok's official OAuth authorization, the app receives an access token that permits uploading video content to that account's draft inbox. We request only the video.upload scope.

What we collect and store

We store only the access and refresh tokens required to upload videos, kept in secured credential storage. We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal TikTok user data, follower information, analytics, messages, or viewer data. We do not use TikTok data for advertising or profiling.

How content is posted

Videos are uploaded to the connected account's TikTok inbox as drafts. No content is published publicly by this application; the account owner reviews and posts each video manually within the TikTok app.

Data retention

Tokens are retained only while the integration is active and are deleted upon disconnection.

Third parties

We do not share data with third parties except the official APIs of the connected platforms (such as TikTok) as required to perform uploads.

Contact
